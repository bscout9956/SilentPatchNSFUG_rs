# Statement of Ethics and AI Usage:

I used AI (namely Gemini) for assistance with low level assembly concepts, as I do not possess all the necessary skills to deal with concepts such as:
- Calling Conventions
- ABI Register/Parameter Conventions
- Deep Compiler Shenanigans
- Specific Machine Targets

I felt that breaking some barriers with the usage of AI would help me push forward and learn a bit about concepts that I'm not familiar yet. 
Or practically, how to implement a hookable DLL in Rust.

In any case, I tried my best to understand and note down the necessary explanations given for me. 
There is no guarantee that portions of code, those assisted by the LLM, will function 100% as the original.
Such instances will be written down as "written by LLM" or something of the type. 

**This is what we have for today.**

I must emphasize that for everything else (in other words, pure Rust code and other concepts), I used my brain.

IMPORTANT: **If this bothers you, feel free to disregard the project.** 
I would rather make it transparent and clear, than to just shadow drop a project like this as if I am some sort of Rust/C/C++/Reverse Engineer genius. I am not.
